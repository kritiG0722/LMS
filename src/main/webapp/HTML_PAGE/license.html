Placement Clause Agreement

1. Eligibility for Placement Assistance Students must successfully complete their enrolled course(s), including all assignments, projects, and evaluations, to be eligible for placement assistance from Visionary IT Global.

2. Minimum Attendance Requirement A minimum of 85% attendance is mandatory throughout the course to qualify for participation in the placement process.

3. Resume and Interview Readiness Students must attend all resume-building sessions, mock interviews, and soft skills training organized by Visionary IT Global to qualify for placement opportunities.

4. Placement Support with Conditions 

Visionary IT Global is committed to helping students land their first job after course completion. However, 
        this support is subject to certain conditions, including:
•  The student has paid the full course fee with no dues remaining.
•  All course modules, projects, and assessments have been completed and passed as per the evaluation criteria.
•  The student has actively participated in resume preparation, mock interviews, and soft skills training sessions.
•  The student agrees to accept job opportunities across any location in India as per industry demand.
•  The student maintains professional behavior during all placement activities. Any non-professional, disrespectful,
 or unethical behavior towards the placement team, trainers, or recruiters will lead to disqualification from the placement process.

5. Company and Role Acceptance PolicyStudents are expected to accept interview calls and job roles aligned with their skill level. Refusal of more than two genuine opportunities may lead to disqualification from further placement support.

6. Code of Conduct During Placement DrivesProfessional behavior and punctuality are expected during all interactions with recruiters. Any misconduct may lead to suspension from the placement cell.

7. Honesty in ApplicationStudents must provide accurate personal, academic, and project details in their resumes. Any falsification may result in blacklisting from placement assistance.

8. Post-Offer RulesOnce a student accepts an offer, they will be considered “placed” and will no longer be eligible for further placement drives unless the offer is revoked by the employer.

9. Feedback and Reporting ObligationStudents are required to provide placement status updates and employer feedback for internal reporting and service improvement purposes.

10. Liability LimitationVisionary IT Global will not be held liable for any recruitment decisions, employment terms, or job discontinuation made by the hiring companies.
